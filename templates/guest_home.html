Know Before You Go!
CrushMaven makes it safe and easy to find out if someone you're attracted to feels the same, without revealing yourself. If the feeling isn't mutual, keep your friendship, and move on!
How It Works
1 Crush Selection
Select a Facebook friend - or any Facebook user - that you're attracted to. We'll send them an anonymous invitation to sign in to CrushMaven. That's right, anonymous!
2 Admirer Lineup
Once signed in, we present your crush with a lineup of potential admirers - similar to a police lineup - with you and nine carefully selected others. It's randomly ordered so your crush won't know who their actual admirer is!
3 Match Alert
Your crush selects any lineup members they're attracted to. If you're picked, we'll notify both parties. And your crush will think they chose you first! Even if you're not picked, we'll let you know, and you identity will remain safe.
Who It Works On
Facebook Friends
Your crush's lineup will only consist of Facebook users who are also friends with your crush on Facebook. Soon CrushMaven will work on your LinkedIn and Twitter contacts as well!
Friends of Friends
Not friends with your crush on Facebook but share mutual Facebook friends? No Problem. Your crush's lineup will only consist of Facebook users who are also friends-of-friends with your crush on Facebook. Works like magic!
Anyone on Facebook
Not friends with your crush on Facebook? Share no mutual friends either? Maybe you saw them in a photo and thought they were cute. No Problem. Just don't them send a random Facebook friend request. That's creepy!
