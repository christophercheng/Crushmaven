Like Someone Select a Facebook friend, or any Facebook user, that you're attracted to. We'll send them an anonymous invitation to sign in.
Admirer Lineup Once signed in, we'll present the person you liked with a lineup of potential admirers - similar to a police lineup - with you and nine carefully selected others in it. They'll identify anyone they're attracted to. Because the lineup is randomly ordered, they won't know who their actual admirer is.
Match Notification If you're picked from the lineup, we'll notify both parties about the match. Your attraction might actually think they chose you first. If not picked, we'll also let you know, and your identity remains protected.
