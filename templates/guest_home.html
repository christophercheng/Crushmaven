How It Works
(1) Crush Selection
Select a Facebook friend - or any Facebook user - that you're attracted to. We send them an anonymous invitation to sign in.
(2) Crush Lineup
Once signed in, we present your crush with a lineup of potential admirers - similar to a police lineup - with you and nine carefully selected others. Because it is randomly ordered, your crush won't know who their actual admirer is.
(3) Match Alert
Your crush selects lineup members they're attracted to. If you're picked, we notify both parties. Your attraction may actually think they chose you first! Even if you're not picked, we let you know, and your identity remains safe.
